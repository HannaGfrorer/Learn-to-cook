Welcome to cook online. We want everyone to be able to cook delicious dishes from different countries. With us, you'll learn everything you need to cook that delicious Italian pizza from last summer's vacation or why not that wok you've been thinking about for a long time.


We offer online courses where you get access to the course material wherever you are or whatever time of day you feel like cooking. You get a recipe and a shopping list of the ingredients you need. Every course consists of an online video where our chef guides you through all the stages of cooking.


During the course's video lessons, you learn theory, technique and cooking. Take the course at your own pace, when it suits you and repeat as many times as you want.
